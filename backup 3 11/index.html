About Us

Equine Solutions is established 2014 in Cairo, Egypt with software and applications developing activities to serve the equine field in administration. It was founded by a group of ex show jumping riders have experience in organizing (Equestrian events ) jumping events and managing the sport affairs our mission is offering new technologies packed with excellent quality of support to serve those who on the Sport Management (Shows Organizers , Federations, Clubs & Riders) our VISION is to get rid from paper works and switch to paperless environment depending on the new technology.

IOC

This system is interested in the smallest details in the art of administrating the International Equestrian Championships starting from the veterinary Inspection and riders declaration at the same time to the start list draw and results reports with prize money and facilitate show tracking by the mobile app which keeps organisers and riders updated with evrything by creating self login to manage his account in the show. IOC comply with all FEI articles so just one step to select an article and it will calculate the results and prize money. you can even print statements for each rider in the finance module and sell your service and products to the riders in easy controllable way
